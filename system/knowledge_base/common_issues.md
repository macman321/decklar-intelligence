## Bluetooth Restrictions
**Issue:** Some customers have facilities that prohibit Bluetooth (BLE) usage.
**Impact:** If BLE is enabled on Bee Labels, devices won't activate or report at restricted sites.
**Resolution:** Always confirm with customer during onboarding: "Do any of your facilities have Bluetooth restrictions?" If yes, disable BLE in device configuration.
